The Golden Hour Lounge

Where the Sea Meets the Stars

Inspired by the serene beauty of Koh Phangan's coastline, Kira Kai blends modern European cuisine with handcrafted cocktails and an understated, boho-chic elegance. Every table is a front-row seat to the horizon.

Signature Sips

Handcrafted cocktails with premium spirits, Asian-inspired flavours, and house infusions.

Coastal Kitchen

Modern European small plates, sharing boards, and mains — crafted daily with seasonal produce.

Artisan Coffee

Locally roasted beans by Rong Cafe. Espresso, flat whites, matcha, and iced specialties.

Golden Hour

Arrive as the sun dips below the horizon. Stay through the magic hour when the sky ignites in amber and gold.
